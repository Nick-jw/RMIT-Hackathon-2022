About Us

We believe in ensuring that every workplace drives equality and fairness. Check how your potential employer stacks up, or review your employer to help individuals make and educated decision.

Why are we doing this?

Women leave stem jobs at twice the rate that men do. We want to reduce this by providing transparency and information regarding companies so both men and women can make educated decisions and work for companies that champion gender equality.

About our Equality Index

Our equality index tracks several key factors that matter for everyone in the workplace. Reports of discrimination, bullying and inequality all negatively affect a companies equality index. Once enough data is collected a companies rating and reviews can be viewed by anyone.
